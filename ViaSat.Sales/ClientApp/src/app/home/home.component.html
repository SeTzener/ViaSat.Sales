ViaSat Sales

This is an exercise to assess the candidate's readiness. It involves transforming a list of items into a receipt including taxes and grand total. In a real project, instead of a hard coded list there would be a form in which to enter the data of the goods to be purchased in order to correctly process the receipt. For example, to understand if the product is exempt from taxes, through a droplist, it would be possible to select the category of the good and from here understand how to tax it.
